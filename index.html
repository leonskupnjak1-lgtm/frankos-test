O namaAbout Us

Više od
pizze. More than
pizza.

Franko's je Zagreb institucija. Nagrađivani pizza bar koji ne kompromitira ni sastojke ni tehniku. Dva stila — hrskava rimska A la Romana i prozračna Napoletana — oba savršena. Franko's is a Zagreb institution. An award-winning pizza bar that never compromises on ingredients or technique. Two styles — crispy Roman A la Romana and airy Neapolitan — both perfect.
